<p class="lead">Buying an electric golf cart is a significant investment. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know — from motor types and battery options to features, pricing, and top recommendations.</p>

## Types of Electric Golf Carts

### Standard Golf Carts
Designed primarily for golf course use. Typically 2-seater with basic features. Range: 20-40 miles.

### 4-Seater Golf Carts
Larger carts that seat 4 adults. Ideal for families, resorts, and commercial use. Range: 40-60+ miles.

### All-Terrain Golf Carts
Built with larger tires, higher ground clearance, and more powerful motors. Perfect for farms, ranches, and off-road use.

### Luxury Golf Carts
Premium models with leather seats, advanced displays, sound systems, and custom paint jobs.

## Key Features to Look For

### Motor Power
- **Entry-level**: 2000-3000W — Flat terrain only
- **Mid-range**: 3000-5000W — Handles hills and light off-road
- **High-power**: 5000W+ — All-terrain capability

### Battery Options
| Type | Lifespan | Charge Time | Cost |
|------|----------|-------------|------|
| Lead-Acid | 3-5 years | 8-10 hours | $ |
| Lithium-Ion | 5-10 years | 4-6 hours | $$$ |
| LiFePO4 | 8-12 years | 3-5 hours | $$$$ |

### Suspension
- **None**: Smooth surfaces only
- **Front only**: Light off-road
- **Full suspension**: All-terrain comfort

### Display & Controls
- Basic LED display
- LCD touch screen
- IPS display (best visibility)
- App connectivity

## Maintenance Tips

1. **Charge regularly** — Don't let the battery fully drain
2. **Check tire pressure** — Monthly checks extend tire life
3. **Clean the cart** — Wash regularly to prevent corrosion
4. **Inspect brakes** — Check brake pads every 6 months
5. **Battery care** — Store in moderate temperatures, use OEM charger

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How fast can an electric golf cart go?
Most golf carts have a top speed of 15-25 mph. Some high-performance models can reach 30+ mph.

### Do I need a license to drive a golf cart?
It depends on your location. Many states require a driver's license for street-legal golf carts. Check local regulations.

### How long does it take to charge a golf cart?
- Lead-acid: 8-10 hours
- Lithium-ion: 4-6 hours
- LiFePO4: 3-5 hours

### Can golf carts handle hills?
Yes, if they have sufficient motor power (3000W+) and appropriate gearing. All-terrain models handle hills best.
