## How to Choose the Best Electric Golf Cart

When shopping for an electric golf cart, consider:

1. **Seating Capacity** — 2-seater for couples, 4-seater for families
2. **Range** — Look for 40+ miles per charge for full-day use
3. **Motor Power** — 3000W+ for hills and all-terrain
4. **Suspension** — Full suspension for off-road comfort
5. **Battery Type** — Lithium batteries last longer and charge faster
6. **Intended Use** — Golf course, farm, resort, or neighborhood

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are electric golf carts street legal?
It depends on your state and local regulations. Many areas allow street-legal golf carts (LSVs) on roads with speed limits under 35 mph. Check your local laws before driving on public roads.

### How long do electric golf cart batteries last?
Most lithium batteries last 5-10 years with proper care. Lead-acid batteries typically last 3-5 years. Always use the OEM charger and avoid fully draining the battery.

### Can I use an electric golf cart on a golf course?
Yes! Most golf courses welcome electric golf carts. They're quiet, produce no emissions, and are easy to operate.

### How much does an electric golf cart cost?
Prices range from $3,000 for basic models to $13,000+ for premium all-terrain carts with advanced features.
